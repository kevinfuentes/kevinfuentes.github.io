A Little Bit About Me...


   Hi! My name is Kevin Fuentes. I am a 6th grader at MAS @ Zelda Glazer and work in a Technical Production class taught by Mr.Pate. I have enjoyed Photoshop so far. Photoshop allows me to create anything I want, and the only limit is my imagination. I have been playing soccer since I was around 5 years old. Playing soccer brought me to a new level of respect and discipline. It also taught me how to work with others and use each members strength to have a better outcome. And how do I connect soccer and Photoshop you may ask? The answer is simple. CREATIVITY. In soccer you have to do creative flicks, passes, and shots in order to work as an effective and powerful team. In photoshop you have to be creative in your projects so that they don't look the same as the others and so that your projects come out better. All in all, I love soccer and Photoshop!
